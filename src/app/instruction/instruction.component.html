How to Apply for an Appointment

  • Step 1: Register an account using your email and create a secure password. Fill in your personal details to complete registration.
  • Step 2: Sign in to your account and access your dashboard, where you will find the "Fix Appointment" option.
  • Step 3: Browse the list of available doctors, select a doctor, and choose a convenient time slot from the available options.
  • Step 4: Confirm your appointment after reviewing the details to finalize the booking.
  • Step 5: View the assigned doctor's details, including their name, specialization, and clinic location.
  • Step 6: Receive appointment confirmation notifications via email or SMS along with reminders.
